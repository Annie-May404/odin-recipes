Chocolate Chip cookies

Description

Baking is a cute hobby of mine, so I decided to share all baking recipes. First up is a chocolate chip cookie recipe, since those are the most iconic baked good in my very biased opinion. Here's a link to the original recipe by Laura. The recipe's easy to follow, and you get soft, chewy cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large egss
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
    • <1i>1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips(12 oz)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius). Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 tablespoons of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes. take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
